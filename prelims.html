The two papers of the IAS prelims are:

Note:⅓ of the total marks allotted to the question will be deducted for every wrong answer
  1. General Studies (100 questions)(200 marks)

    2. The General Studies test is the first paper of the preliminary examination. This test include: Indian Polity, Geography, History, Indian Economy, Science and Technology, Environment and Ecology, International Relations and associated UPSC current affairs.

  2. Civil Services Aptitude Test (CSAT)(80 Questions)(200 marks)

    3. This UPSC Prelims syllabus for CSAT intends to assess the aptitude of the candidate in solving ‘Reasoning and Analytical’ questions, apart from ‘Reading Comprehension’ and the occasionally asked ‘Decision Making’ questions. The ‘Decision Making’ based questions are generally exempt from negative marks.
